## Base Character Description
The Sappio Orb is a cute, friendly glowing sphere character with:
- Soft cyan/teal color (#a8d5d5)
- Gentle glow and luminescence
- Orbital rings that circle around it
- Simple, minimalist design
- Dark background for contrast
- Friendly, approachable personality

## Technical Specifications

### Image Requirements
- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Standard Size:** 256x256px
- **Mini Size:** 128x128px (for pack-card variants)
- **Color Palette:** 
  - Primary: #a8d5d5 (cyan/teal)
  - Secondary: #8bc5c5 (darker teal)
  - Accent: #f5e6d3 (warm cream)
- **Background:** Dark/transparent
- **Style:** Digital art, clean, modern, friendly

### Optimization Steps
1. Generate image at specified size
2. Ensure transparency/alpha channel
3. Export as PNG
4. Optimize with TinyPNG or similar tool
5. Optionally convert to WebP for production
6. Place in `/public/orb/` directory

### Consistency Guidelines
- Maintain the same orb character design across all poses
- Keep the cyan/teal color consistent (#a8d5d5)
- Use dark backgrounds for all images
- Ensure orbital rings are visible but not overwhelming
- Keep expressions friendly and approachable
- Avoid harsh shadows or overly complex details
- Ensure images are clear and recognizable at small sizes
